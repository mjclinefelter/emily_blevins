    Benefits

    Benefits of music therapy are wide ranging and very individualized. However, here are some examples of the positive outcomes of music therapy.

    Physical

    • Maintain or enhance motor skills
    • Maintain or improve physical stamina
    • Support for post-stroke rehabilitation
    • Maintain or enhance cognitive abilities
    • Decreased pain perception

    Emotional

    • Opportunity for emotional validation, and reminiscence
    • Decrease depression, anxiety, and agitation
    • Increased creativity
    • Emotional support
    • Learn and practice effective coping strategies
    • Identify and express emotions

    Social

    • Enhance social connections with peers
    • Decrease feelings of isolation
    • Increased self-expression
