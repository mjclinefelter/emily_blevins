    • Private One-to-One Therapy

    Meet with your music therapist in your own home, whether in a private residence or in a senior living facility to address individualized goals based on your needs, strengths, and preferences.

    Goals

    Each situation is unique, but typical goals for private therapy include:

    • Maintain or improve motor skills and physical stamina
    • Support for post-stroke rehabilitation
    • Maintain or enhance cognitive abilities
    • Increased self-expression and creativity
    • Decreased pain perception
    • Decreased depression
    • Decreased anxiety
    • Emotional support to cope with grief, loss, and change associated with this stage of life

    Please contact me to request an initial evaluation for private music therapy services.
